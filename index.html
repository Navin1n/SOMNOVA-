The ritual

From half-remembered to unforgettable

01

Wake & speak

Eyes still closed, trigger recording with a triple-tap on the back of your phone. Just talk — dreams fade in minutes, your voice is faster than any keyboard.

02

It listens

Your words are written down and read for symbols, feelings, and the single most vivid moment of the dream.

03

It paints

That moment becomes a short, dreamlike vertical film — soft light, deep indigo, moonlit gold. Your dream, the way it felt.

04

It stays

Every film joins your private archive. Recurring symbols surface over time — the language your dreams keep speaking.
