Privacy policy

Last updated 11 July 2026

Seluna records your dreams and turns them into short films and interpretations. Dreams are intimate, so this policy is short and plain.

What we collect

  • Account: an anonymous account identifier. No email or phone number is required. If you later add an email to protect your archive, we store it for sign-in only.
  • Your sign & optional birth details: the zodiac sign you choose; optionally your birth date, time, and place if you add them to deepen the astrological reading.
  • Preferences: who the dreamer in your films is (woman / man / they-them) and your chosen dream perspective.
  • Dreams: the audio you record, its transcript, the generated video and interpretation, and the symbols found in it.
  • Purchases: subscriptions are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat. We receive your subscription status tied to your anonymous account id — never your card details.
  • Device: an optional push-notification token so we can tell you when your dream is ready. Crash reports (Sentry) contain no dream content and no personal identifiers.

How your dream is processed

To create your result, your dream audio and text are sent to external AI providers: transcription and content moderation (OpenAI), interpretation (Anthropic), and image/video generation (fal.ai or Replicate). These providers process your dream content to produce your film and reading. We send only what is needed — never your identity alongside it.

What we never do

  • We never sell your data or share it with advertisers.
  • Your dreams are never public and are never used to train our own models.
  • No one else can access your dreams: access is enforced per-account at the database level.

Where it lives

Your data is stored with Supabase (database and file storage) behind row-level security scoped to your account. Dream audio lives in a private bucket only your account can read.

Deleting everything

In the app: Account → Delete account. This permanently removes your account, every dream, all generated media, and your audio files. It cannot be undone. You can also email us at the address below.

Not advice

Interpretations are for reflection and entertainment only — they are not medical, psychological, or clinical advice.
