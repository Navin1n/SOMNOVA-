Support

Seluna turns the dream you speak on waking into a short film and a gentle four-lens reading. If something isn't working, or you have a question, we're here.

Contact

Email support@flowbeats.net — include the word Seluna in the subject and we'll get back to you within a couple of days.

Common questions

  • My dream is taking a while. Films take a few minutes to paint. You can close the app — we'll keep working, and the dream appears in your archive when it's ready.
  • A dream didn't finish. A failed dream never uses up your day — open it and try again, or record a fresh take.
  • Managing or cancelling your subscription. Subscriptions are handled by Apple: in the app go to Account → Manage subscription, or open Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your iPhone.
  • Keeping your archive safe. Add an email under Account → Your archive so your dreams can be recovered on a new phone.
  • Deleting your account. Account → Delete account removes your account, every dream, and all generated media, permanently.
  • Privacy. How your dreams are handled, in plain words: the privacy policy.
