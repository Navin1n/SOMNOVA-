The daytime habit

Reality-check reminders

Three silent nudges a day: look at your hands, re-read a line of text, ask how you got here. In dreams these tend to warp. Done with real doubt, the habit follows you across the threshold of sleep — and one night the answer to "am I dreaming?" is yes. Somnova schedules them for you; ignore them freely, honour them when you can.

Before sleep

MILD — set an intention

As you drift off, replay a recent dream and repeat one sentence: next time I'm dreaming, I'll notice I'm dreaming. The most-studied induction method there is.

For committed nights

WBTB — wake back to bed

Wake five hours in, stay softly awake for twenty minutes with your dream signs, then return to sleep holding your intention. You go straight back into long REM — the easiest doorway.

Had one? Tap "I knew I was dreaming" on the dream. Your lucid count grows in your practice hub — proof the training is working.
